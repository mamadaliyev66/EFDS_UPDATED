How It Works

The technology behind our early fire detection system

1. Video Feed Analysis

The system continuously analyzes video feeds from connected cameras, looking for visual signatures of fire.

  • Processes frames in real-time
  • Detects flame patterns and smoke
  • Works with most IP cameras

2. AI Processing

Deep learning models evaluate potential fire signatures to confirm threats and reduce false alarms.

  • Convolutional Neural Networks for image analysis
  • Trained on thousands of fire/non-fire images
  • Continuous learning improves accuracy

3. Alert System

When fire is confirmed, the system triggers multiple alert mechanisms to ensure rapid response.

  • Visual and audible alarms on premises
  • Push notifications to mobile devices
  • Email alerts with incident details

About This Project

The Early Fire Detection System was developed to address the critical need for faster, more reliable fire detection in various environments.

Traditional smoke detectors can be slow to respond and are prone to false alarms. Our computer vision-based solution identifies visual signatures of fire much earlier in the development cycle, providing valuable time for response and evacuation.
